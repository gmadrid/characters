Roleplaying Guide
The core fiction
The magic belongs to Goldmaximus. Not to Tim. Tim is just nearby when it happens. The theater fire was Goldmaximus. All surges are Goldmaximus. Tim is an innocent bystander who happens to be wearing an ancient dragon on his left hand.
Playing the puppet
Consult Goldmaximus before significant decisions — hold him up, whisper, nod seriously at the response. Blame him immediately when anything goes wrong. Credit him when anything goes right. He demands to be addressed by full title. When a Wild Magic Surge fires, turn to the puppet first. Let him deny everything.
The thing never discussed
At the bottom of his pack: a small singed flyer. "The Great Andrini — One Night Only." He kept it when he fled. Goldmaximus knows about it and has the good grace not to bring it up.
What others see
Some think the puppet is a magic item. Some think he's being silly. Some are genuinely uncertain. Do not resolve this. Let each person form their own read. The ambiguity is the game.
His real goal
He says he wants a cure. What he actually wants is to understand what happened that night. Some part of him — the performer, the trickster — finds it fascinating even as it terrifies him. He hasn't admitted that yet.
Combat Priority
1
Enemy just succeeded on a roll?
Silvery Barbs as a reaction. Force the reroll, give an ally advantage. Costs your reaction and a 1st slot — usually worth it.
2
3+ enemies clustered?
Fireball. 8d6, DC 15 DEX. Average 28 damage. Confirm fighters are clear — it fills from a point and goes around corners.
3
Fight going badly?
Hypnotic Pattern. 30ft cube, WIS DC 15, incapacitates on fail. Fight-ender. Requires concentration — stay out of melee.
4
Fighter about to swing?
Bend Luck (reaction, 2 sorcery pts) adds 1d4 to their roll. Or hold Silvery Barbs for when they miss and use it to force the reroll.
5
Single target, conserving slots?
Fire Bolt cantrip (+7, 2d10). Or Chaos Bolt (1st slot) for the chance it chains on matching d8s.
6
Someone grabbed you?
Shocking Grasp (advantage vs metal armor, prevents their OA) then Misty Step bonus action to escape 30ft. War Caster lets you cast Shocking Grasp as an OA too.
Slot economy
3rd (2): Fireball or Hypnotic Pattern only  ·  2nd (3): Misty Step or Shatter  ·  1st (4): Silvery Barbs or Chaos Bolt  ·  Sorcery pts (5): Bend Luck; convert to slots only in emergencies
