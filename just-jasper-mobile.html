Who He Is
Real name
Tim Anders · formerly The Great Andrini · now Just Jasper
What happened
Street magician. During a show, real magic manifested. The theater burned. He fled under a false name and has been traveling as a street performer since.
The fiction he maintains
The magic belongs to Goldmaximus the Gold, Ancient Dragon of the Golden Flame, He Who Is Also Quite Gold. Not to Tim. Tim is just nearby when it happens.
The truth
It's a sock. He knows it's a sock. He cannot afford to know that.
Personality
Traits
Consults Goldmaximus before any significant decision. Every interaction is a little bit of a show.
Ideal
Understanding. I need to know what happened that night. Just to know.
Bond
Somewhere there's a theater with my name on a scorched poster.
Flaw
The magic is Goldmaximus's. It is not mine. That's final.
Playing Goldmaximus
When to consult him
Before significant decisions — hold him up, whisper, nod seriously at the response.
When a surge happens
Turn to the puppet first. Let him deny everything.
His full title
Goldmaximus the Gold, Ancient Dragon of the Golden Flame, He Who Is Also Quite Gold. He expects the full title in formal situations.
The singed flyer
Bottom of the pack. "The Great Andrini — One Night Only." He kept it when he fled. Never discussed. Goldmaximus knows and has the grace not to bring it up.
